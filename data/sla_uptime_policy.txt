SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA) & UPTIME POLICY
==============================================
Effective: January 2025

1. UPTIME COMMITMENT
---------------------
  Starter Plan     — 99.5% monthly uptime
  Pro Plan         — 99.9% monthly uptime
  Enterprise Plan  — 99.95% monthly uptime (custom SLA available)

Uptime is measured over a rolling calendar month, excluding scheduled maintenance.

2. SCHEDULED MAINTENANCE
-------------------------
  - Routine maintenance occurs on the THIRD SUNDAY of each month, 2 AM–4 AM UTC.
  - Emergency maintenance windows are announced via the Status Page and email
    at least 1 hour in advance where operationally possible.
  - Scheduled maintenance windows do NOT count against uptime calculations.

3. INCIDENT RESPONSE TIMES
---------------------------
  Severity   Definition                     First Response    Resolution Target
  ---------  -----------------------------  ----------------  -----------------
  P1 Critical  Full service outage          15 minutes        4 hours
  P2 High      Major feature degradation    1 hour            8 hours
  P3 Medium    Partial feature impact       4 hours           24 hours
  P4 Low       Minor cosmetic/UX issues     1 business day    5 business days

4. SLA CREDITS
--------------
  If monthly uptime falls below the commitment, customers are eligible for
  service credits applied to the next invoice:

  Uptime Achieved    Credit (% of Monthly Fee)
  ----------------   --------------------------
  99.0% – 99.49%     10%
  95.0% – 98.99%     25%
  Below 95.0%        50%

  To request an SLA credit:
  - Submit a request within 30 days of the incident.
  - Email sla-credits@platform.io with your Account ID and incident reference.
  - Credits cannot be converted to cash.

5. SUPPORT CHANNELS AND SLAs
------------------------------
  Channel              Availability         Response SLA
  -------------------  -------------------  -----------------
  In-App Chat          Mon–Fri 9 AM–8 PM    Under 2 minutes (live)
  Email Support        24/7                 < 4 business hours
  Phone (Enterprise)   Mon–Fri 9 AM–6 PM    Immediate
  Status Page          Always               Real-time updates

  Status Page URL: status.platform.io

6. EXCLUSIONS
--------------
  The following are excluded from SLA calculations:
  - Customer-caused outages (misconfigured API calls, exceeding rate limits).
  - Third-party service failures (AWS, Stripe, Twilio).
  - Force majeure events (natural disasters, cyberattacks beyond our control).
  - Issues reported but not reproducible by our engineering team.

7. ENTERPRISE CUSTOM SLAs
--------------------------
  Enterprise customers may negotiate custom SLA terms including:
  - Higher uptime guarantees (up to 99.99%)
  - Dedicated support engineers
  - Proactive monitoring and runbook execution
  - Financial penalties (not just credits) for breaches

  Contact your Customer Success Manager or sales@platform.io.

8. REPORTING AN INCIDENT
-------------------------
  Real-time incident tracking: status.platform.io
  Report an undetected issue: support@platform.io (include timestamps, account ID,
  affected feature, and observed vs expected behaviour).
