BILLING POLICY — SaaS Platform Support
=======================================
Last Updated: January 2025

1. SUBSCRIPTION PLANS
---------------------
We offer three subscription tiers:

  Starter    — $29/month  — Up to 5 users, 60 API req/min, 10 GB storage
  Pro        — $99/month  — Up to 25 users, 300 API req/min, 100 GB storage
  Enterprise — Custom     — Unlimited users, custom rate limits, dedicated SLA

All plans are billed monthly or annually. Annual billing provides a 20% discount.

2. PAYMENT METHODS
------------------
Accepted payment methods:
  - Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
  - Bank transfer (Enterprise plans only)
  - PayPal

Payment is collected on the first day of each billing cycle. Failed payments
trigger a 7-day grace period before service suspension.

3. INVOICES
-----------
Invoices are emailed automatically on the billing date to the registered
billing email address. To change the billing email:
  Dashboard → Account → Billing → Update Billing Email

Invoice history is available under Dashboard → Billing → Invoice History.

4. REFUND POLICY
----------------
4.1 Monthly Subscriptions
  Refunds are NOT issued for partial months already in use.
  Cancellation takes effect at the END of the current billing period.

4.2 Annual Subscriptions
  A pro-rated refund is available within the first 30 days of the annual term.
  After 30 days, no refund is issued for remaining months.

4.3 Exceptional Cases
  Billing disputes due to platform outages exceeding 4 hours are reviewed
  case-by-case by our billing team. Submit a dispute via:
  support@platform.io with subject line "BILLING DISPUTE — [Account ID]"

5. PLAN UPGRADES AND DOWNGRADES
--------------------------------
  Upgrade: Takes effect immediately. You are charged a pro-rated amount for
           the remainder of the current cycle.
  Downgrade: Takes effect at the start of the next billing cycle.
             Data exceeding the new plan limits is archived (not deleted) for
             30 days after downgrade.

6. CANCELLATION
---------------
  Cancel any time from Dashboard → Account → Subscription → Cancel.
  After cancellation:
    - Access continues until end of the paid period.
    - All data is retained for 30 days, then permanently deleted.
    - Export your data BEFORE the retention period expires.

7. TAXES
--------
  Applicable sales tax or VAT is added based on your billing address at
  checkout. Tax-exempt organisations must submit a valid exemption certificate
  via support@platform.io before their next billing cycle.

8. DISPUTED OR DUPLICATE CHARGES
----------------------------------
  If you see a charge you do not recognise:
  Step 1 — Check if a team member upgraded the account.
  Step 2 — Review invoice history in the dashboard.
  Step 3 — Contact billing support within 60 days of the charge:
            Email: billing@platform.io
            Phone: 1-800-555-0199 (Mon–Fri, 9 AM–6 PM EST)

  Disputes submitted after 60 days may not be eligible for review.

9. ENTERPRISE CUSTOM CONTRACTS
-------------------------------
  Enterprise customers with custom MSAs should refer to the contract terms
  for SLA credits, payment schedules, and refund eligibility.
  Contact your dedicated Customer Success Manager for contract-related queries.
